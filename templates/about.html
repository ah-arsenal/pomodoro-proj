The Pomodoro Clock

The pomodoro clock is used to break up work on tasks into manageable 25 minute intervals,
where a break is scheduled upon completion of a successful 25 minute interval of work.
To use the timer, enter your task name in the "Enter Task Name" form box and press start.
The timer will start. When the timer runs out, the task is recorded in your history as a success.
If you leave the page before the timer runs out, the task is recorded as a failure.
You can see your history of successful and failed pomodoros (last 10 only shown) in the history tab.
Thanks for using and happy pomodoro-ing!
